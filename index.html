Goldbach's Conjecture Validation

This site presents a public demonstration of the validity of Goldbach's Conjecture for very large even numbers using the Hybrid GPS Method.

For each even number \( E \), a prime number \( p \) is displayed such that \( q = E - p \) is also prime. We have tested values from \( E = 10^2 \) up to \( E = 10^{10,000} \). The results confirm the validity of the conjecture over this huge range.
