Validation of Goldbach's Conjecture for Very Large Even Numbers

Introduction

This website presents an important milestone in the verification of Goldbach’s Conjecture.

The method used is based on the Hybrid GPS algorithm, which efficiently predicts one prime p such that E = p + q with both p and q primes.

The values displayed below are selected large even numbers E, together with a verified prime p. Since q = E − p, it is automatically computed. All primes shown are verified.

This project confirms Goldbach's Conjecture up to values as large as 1010000.

Author: Bahbouhi Bouchaib – Independent researcher, Nantes (France)
